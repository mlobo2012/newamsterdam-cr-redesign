Since 1984

Building With Purpose

For over 40 years, New Amsterdam Construction & Restoration has been a trusted name in the Hudson Valley. Founded by Bruce van de Veerdonk, our company was built on the principle that quality craftsmanship and customer satisfaction go hand in hand.

Our staff is highly qualified and are certified experts in the industry. From the restoration of the iconic Rockefeller Estates at Kykuit to custom residential builds and timber frame construction, we bring the same commitment to excellence to every project.

Whether you envision a new home, a historic restoration, or a complete remodel, we have the experience, skill, and passion to bring your vision to life — on time and on budget.
